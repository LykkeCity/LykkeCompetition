Lykke Wallet’s Private key distributed backup results
$500
Category: Technology
Creator: Elena Borskaya
Period: Apr 10 – Apr 25 ⋅ 15 days
Participants: 14
Results: 12
Winners: 4

Every Lykke client has his / her own digital key that saves all the money kept in the Wallet. But the client may occasionally lose it, as well as backups of the digital key.

Michael Nikulin · August 30, 2016 · 18 comments

Results of the stage 1. Five solutions were proposed at the stage 1. Please read and learn the solutions and the comments carefully.

Result #1
by Mark Harris

Lykke is already using the 12-word backup system. The Trezor device greatly reduces the range of possible users. We want to ensure safe and secure storage of keys on mobile devices. Transactions are also signed by clients on their mobile devices without any additional devices such as Trezor. One of the important requirements of the contest is to use Lykke client-server architecture. This solution was rejected as being inconsistent with the requirements of the contest.

Result #2
by Leroy Cox

A good solution that won the first prize. But the participant did not show how the split secrets would be passed on to the clients through the Lykke server, so that Lykke couldn't recognize those secrets.
